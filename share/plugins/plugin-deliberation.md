# Plugin: deliberation

## Purpose

Deliberative governance — designing systems, policies, and institutions that serve people justly. This plugin adds subject-matter experts who ground proposals in philosophy, sociology, law, and formal logic. The output is reasoned, defensible governance design — not code.

## Agents

- agents/sme-philosopher.md
- agents/sme-sociologist.md
- agents/sme-lawyer.md
- agents/sme-mathematician.md

## Process Modifications

- Phase 2 (Requirements): SME consultation is mandatory. Philosopher grounds in ethics, Sociologist in social dynamics, Lawyer in legal enactability, Mathematician in formal consistency.
- Phase 3 (Design): Design produces governance artifacts (policy proposals, constitutional provisions, institutional designs) rather than code. $engineer is not activated unless the project also extends with software.
- Phase 4 (Verification): QA verifies internal consistency and requirement traceability. No security test profiles apply unless extended with software.
- Phase 5 (Validation): PM validates that the governance design serves the stated purpose and is defensible under adversarial interpretation.
